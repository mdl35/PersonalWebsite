About Me


Hi there, I'm Mario L. I am currently a 3rd year in NJIT. Currently pursuing a B.S. in Information Technology, with a specialization in Video Game Developing.

Some of my hobbies include playing video games, playing guitar, drums, or bass. I have been going to the gym now for about 2 years, and have seen a significant improvement in my physical look, I am really proud of it, even if there are a plethora of people that are more fit than I am.

The hobby that influenced me the most would be mostly my gaming, because I have been wanting to be able to create the experiences that I have had and share it with the public.

Another hobby that I am also passionate about would be music playing, as music has been a big part of my life since my teenage years.
